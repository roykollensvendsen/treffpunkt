# Signing in

Treffpunkt uses your Google account, so your results are tied to you across
competitions.

## Sign in
1. Open the app — you'll see a **Sign in with Google** button.
2. Tap it and choose your Google account.
3. You're in: the target screen appears.

If sign-in is cancelled or fails, you stay on the sign-in screen and can try
again.

## "Open in Safari or Chrome" (iOS)

Google only allows sign-in from a real browser. If you open the Treffpunkt link
**inside another app's browser** — e.g. tapping it in Messenger, Facebook or
Instagram — Google shows a *"403: disallowed_useragent"* error. The app detects
this and shows an **"Åpne i Safari eller Chrome"** notice with a *Kopier lenke*
button: copy the link, open Safari or Chrome yourself, paste it, and sign in
there.

If you earlier **added Treffpunkt to your home screen**, remove that icon and
add it again — the updated app opens the home-screen shortcut in Safari, where
sign-in works.

## Sign out
Use the sign-out icon in the top-right of the app bar to return to the sign-in
screen. Your session is remembered, so you won't need to sign in every time.
